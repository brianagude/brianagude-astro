  { subtitle: "CMS Architecture", text: "I built a modular Sanity CMS tailored to the client’s needs. This included reusable blocks, structured content relationships, and a content hub capable of referencing information across the site.   { subtitle: "Component Design & Reusability", text: "I implemented a modular frontend with consistent props and strong typing.   { subtitle: "Performance & Rendering", text: "Performance was a major focus, particularly for content-heavy pages. I optimized image loading, preloaded hero assets, and carefully balanced caching strategies using static generation (SSG) and server-side rendering (SSR) where appropriate. Trade-offs were made to preserve image resolution while maintaining page speed." },
  { subtitle: "Collaboration & Iteration", text: "I worked closely with designers and the PM, providing daily updates and weekly summaries on progress, blockers, and priorities.   { subtitle: "Testing & Launch", text: "The final build was rigorously tested across devices and browsers for responsiveness, performance, and CMS functionality.
